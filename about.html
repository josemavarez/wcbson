Welcome To

West palm Beach

School of nursing

We’re a student-centered school focusing on helping our students start and complete their programs of study. Our goal is to help our students start and finish their programs of study. Still, we also believe that success is a partnership.

We’re committed to helping you reach your goals. That’s why we offer flexible, convenient healthcare programs in Professional Nursing Programs. These state-of-the-art programs will allow you to grow personally and professionally through your studies.

We offer a variety of programs that are designed to help you start and finish your college degree. With our flexible schedules, convenient locations, and affordable tuition options, we’re confident that we can find the right program for you.

We are currently in the ACCSC accreditation process.

West Palm Beach’ Nursing program is committed to becoming a program of excellence within the State of Florida.
